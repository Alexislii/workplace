Contact me

I always welcome friends to my place. We can chill and enjoy great food together. You can text me, for sure. But you can also try making appointments here. Let's try something new!

First things first, there are some rules you need to know:

  1. Bring a nice bottle of wine (ideally more than 50 bucks).
  2. If you can't bring a bottle of nice wine, bring a bottle of any wine.
  3. If you can't bring a bottle of wine, bringing a bottle of water is fine.
  4. If you can't bring a bottle of water, just come.

Great! If you accept to follow the rule(s), please make an appointment here.
