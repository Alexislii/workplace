Time to Eat🍛

> 20 mins beef
Steak Oh Steak

I can't say I'm the master of beef. But I try to make this recipe family friendly. I mean, you don't need too many ingredients or cooking techniques to finish it. And it tastes great!

> 20 mins pork
Sweet & Sour Ribs

This is a traditional Chinese dish that almost every Chinese family has their own recipe. Well unlike sweet and sour chicken, you don't need to fry the ribs. Instead, you put them in the Instantpot and wait until the rib becomes very tender and juicy.

< 20 mins seafood
Seafood Fried Rice

What do you do with the left-over rice? They are the best type of rice for fried rice. I will teach you how to make a fast and delicious seafood fried rice in this recipe, and you can use your own ingredients, too!

> 20 mins
Pesto Pasta

Inspired by the great chef Gennaro Contaldo, the pesto is made with a generous amount of good-quality olive oil, nuts, fresh pesto, some garlics, and parmesan cheese. It takes some time, but you will love your hand-made pesto!

< 20 mins beef
Korean Budae-jjigae

It's hot pot in Korean style. Super simple, and a lot of combinations can be done, depending on what ingredients you have.

> 20 mins beef
Beef Noodles

Tomato beef stew is magical. It’s the ideal soup base for a bowl of flavorful beef noodle. Perfect for winter. Just remember: if the beef is too lean, it won’t taste as good in the stew.
